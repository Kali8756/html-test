    About Our LIM

    LOTUS INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT ( LIM )

    Lotus Group of Institutions, Bareilly is among the pioneer institute in Bareilly region since 2007. The institute is continuously and sincerely engaged in imparting the quality management Education to all sections. The institute is trying to accelerate the process of socio-economic growth through quality education consonant with the changing requirements of society in general and industry in particular. Special efforts are being made to realize the vision of the institute by inculcating professional and ethical values in our students.

    The Institute is currently offering various courses as: 2 yrs Full-Time Master of Business Administration (MBA) affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Kalam Technical University, Lucknow,4 Years Full Time Bachelor of Pharmacy.(B.PHARM) And 2 Years Full Time Diploma in Pharmacy(D.PHARM) Affiliated to BTE, Lucknow. 3 yrs Full Time Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.), Bachelor of Science (PCM) Bachelor of Science(ZBC), and affiliated to M.J.P. Rohilkhand University, Bareilly.
